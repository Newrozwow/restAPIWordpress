Super Easy Sauteed Cale

By Leah Rodrigues

Sauteed kale with garlic and lemon is one of the easiest side dishes you'll ever cook. Garlic is heated in a skillet with olive oil before the kale is added. A squeeze of fresh lemon juice at the end adds acidity and flavor to these leafy green vegetables. This recipe works great as a base for many sauteed kale dishes. Feel free to add stewed tomatoes, white beans, mushrooms, onions and any other ingredients that inspire you.
